About Me

Hello, thank you for visiting my site and checking out some of my work. My career thus far has taken me many places and allowed me to be part of some amazing teams. I started my career as a Geographic Information Systems(GIS) Analyst in Syracuse, NY. Three years later I decided to break out and visit China. I lived in Beijing China for four years teaching English and continuing working in GIS part-time. After I returned to the states I spent a little more time in GIS before beginning work at SkilledUp. There I learned about a cornucopia of resources for learning web development. A year and change later I decided to attend Fullstack Academy of Code. I spent six months there in their Immersive Coding Bootcamp learning the ins and outs of the MEAN stack as well as a handful of other technologies. Please take a look at the web dev projects I have worked on so far. Thanks again!
